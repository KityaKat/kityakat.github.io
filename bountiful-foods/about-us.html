Our Story

In 1998, two college friends with a passion for fresh foods, surfing, and community started a business making smoothies out of the kitchen of their apartment. Eventually, that turned into a food truck, which turned into a fleet of food trucks. 25 years later, we still run Bountiful Foods together, and we still have the same passions which led us to start this business. With food trucks present on beaches and at farmer's markets throughout Los Angeles, Orange and San Diego counties, Bountiful Foods is here for all your organic, fresh produce needs. Whether you're a local, or a tourist just here for the day, stop by and see us!

Our Promise

Supporting us isn't just about supporting our business. We've worked hard to cultivate a community of small, local growers, including many community gardens throughout the area. We believe in following organic growing practices and so do all our grower-partners. We think our customers deserve the best we can provide, and we take that charge very seriously. Additionally, our dedication to preserving the costal areas we love means that we've also partnered with The Coastal Conservancy of California to give back to the places that shaped us. Ten percent of all proceeds go to support the conservancy's work.

Our Mission

To provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas.
