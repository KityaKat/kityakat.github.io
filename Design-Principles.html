Design Principles

White Space

DFTBA Records

DFTBA Records is a company that partners with internet creators to help them sell merch. There website shows a good use of whitespace, and demonstates a clean design aesthetic.

Fitts's Law

Castbox

Castbox is a podcast platform. Their goal is to get you to listen to podcasts using their app. Therefore, according to Fitts's Law, their call to action button is relatively large, placed in an easily accessed area and a contrasting color. All of this draws your attention to it and makes it easier to use.

Contrast

Zillow

Zillow is a website that hosts real estate listings. There good use of contrast makes their site easy to understand and use.
