Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

DFTBA Records

DFTBA Records is an online company that partners with internet creators to help them sell merch. This shot shows a good use of Visual Hierarchy to draw attention to their announcement of moving to a new warehouse

Fitts's Law

Castbox

Castbox is a podcast platform. Their goal is to get you to listen to podcasts using their app. Therefore, according to Fitts's Law, their call to action button is relatively large, placed in an easily accessed area and in a contrasting color. All of this draws your attention to it and makes it easier to use.

Contrast

Zillow Landing Page

Zillow is a website that hosts real estate listings. Their good use of contrast makes their site easy to understand and use.
