Audience

Audience

This website is intended for those who 1) live in the Cy-Fair area of Texas, and 2) either own a business, or are interested in owning a business.

Personas

1) Sandra Burton is a 28 year old wife, and mother of three. She's also a CPA who has quit her corporate job to be more available for her children, but she's still interested in working freelance to bring in her own income. She might use the Chamber's website to make business contacts or see what kinds of licensure or pemits she may need to go into business for herself.

2) Ivan Silva is 40 years old, and a local restaurateur. Ivan wants to expand his business, but lacks resources and business contacts. He might use the website for information about joining the chamber, and the benefits being a member could have for his business.

Scenarios

  • Checking for New business openings
  • Getting weather updates
  • Finding networking events
  • Looking up job listings
  • Finding information about opening a business
